Our Story


In 1998, we aimed to revive the classic cake amidst Singapore's trend of complex French pastries, focusing on simplicity with our single offering: a dark chocolate cake with layers of fudge.

Now, even though we've expanded our menu to include a vast array of cakes, ice creams, truffles, chocolate bars, and cookies, and have launched restaurants with chocolate-centric buffets and drinks, our Original All Chocolate Cake remains a beloved favorite

Proudly Singapore's favorite chocolate destination, we're grateful for our journey from a single cake to a chocolate haven.
